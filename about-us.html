Rose water is well-known for its skin-soothing and hydrating properties. It is often used as a facial toner to balance the skin's pH, reduce redness, and refresh the complexion. It can also be added to facial masks and skincare products.

Rose petal water has a delightful floral fragrance that makes it a popular choice for aromatherapy and as a natural perfume. Many people use it as a room or linen spray to create a calming and pleasant atmosphere.
